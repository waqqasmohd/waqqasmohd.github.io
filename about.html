About

Descrii, Description Generator generates unique and great quality product descriptions for your store.

Merchants leveraging platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce and Volusion to create an online eCommerce presence face challenges on a daily basis when it comes to product content. From being inexperienced in the eCommerce landscape, to working with an overwhelming volume of SKUs, the quality and consistency of copywriting can suffer. Brand and platform standards are often misused, and the content creation process yields low-quality results at an excessive cost.

As more consumers turn to the web to research and purchase items on their shopping lists, product content has become increasingly important. For eCommerce sellers with larger catalogues, creating rich product descriptions can be time-consuming and costly. Until now.

Utilizing Artificial Intelligence (AI), this content writing software automatically transforms raw product specifications into engaging, unique, optimized content.With our advanced NLP solution, you can deliver added value to your users by offering them Descrii’s powerful content generation capabilities. When used by your customers, this content writing software automatically creates thousands of unique product descriptions leveraging desired brand standards and delivering a consistency in quality visitors will grow to expect from an eCommerce website.
